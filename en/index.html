FAQ

What is the Matrix of Destiny?

The Matrix of Destiny is a self-discovery method based on the 22 energies of the Major Arcana of Tarot. Using your date of birth, key positions are calculated: personality, soul, purpose, karma, talents, and other aspects of your life path.

How to calculate the Matrix of Destiny?

Only a date of birth is needed. The day, month and year numbers are reduced to the 1-22 range, then additional positions are calculated using special formulas.

Is the calculation free?

Yes, the basic Destiny Matrix calculation on our website is completely free. You will receive a full matrix with all positions and a brief description of each energy.

What do the numbers in the Matrix of Destiny mean?

Each number from 1 to 22 corresponds to one of the Major Arcana of Tarot. For example, 1 — The Magician (leadership, will), 2 — The High Priestess (intuition, wisdom), 3 — The Empress (creativity, abundance).

How is the Destiny Matrix different from numerology?

The Destiny Matrix works with 22 energies (Major Arcana of Tarot), while classical numerology works with numbers 1-9. The Matrix provides a more detailed and multifaceted picture.
